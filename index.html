How to Play

In this game, you will test your absolute or relative pitch recognition across the entire 12-tone chromatic scale. The reference point for this game is always C. You’ll hear a random chromatic note from within your chosen range. Identify the note using the answer buttons or the virtual keyboard. Use Replay Note or 'R' to hear the note again, and Next or Space for the next note. You can use the reference button to hear middle C (C4) at any time to re-orient your ear. You can change the settings anytime in the Game Settings menu. You can also download a score card .png should you like!

Game Settings ⚙️

Choose your note range, how many notes to include, and your preferred input style. Note, applying new settings will restart the game and reset the score!
